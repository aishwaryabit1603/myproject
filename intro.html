MEANING :

The Internet of things (IoT) describes the network of physical objects that are embedded with sensors, software, and other technologies for the purpose of connecting and exchanging data with other
devices and systems over the Internet.

EXAMPLES OF WE USE IOT IN OUR DAILY LIVES
  1. Smart appliances (stoves, refrigerators, washers and dryers, coffee machines, slow cookers)
  2. Smart security systems, smart locks, and smart doorbells
  3. Smart home hubs (that control lighting, home heating and cooling, etc.)
  4. Smart assistants (like Amazon Alexa or Apple’s Siri)
  5. Fitness trackers, sleep trackers, and smart scales
